Official Event Die Reference

Dice 1–3 are Scoring Laws. They apply to pegging, hands, and crib.

Dice 4–6 are Royal Decrees — triggers Crown Cut. They resolve after drafting cards.

Leftover Knight’s Table cards: after the draft, all leftover public cards go naturally to the crib so the crib has 4 cards before the Starter Card and 5 cards when scored.

Flush: 4-card flush scores 4 in a hand only. 5-card flush scores 5 in a hand or crib; crib needs all 5 cards to match.

5 of a Kind: 20 points normally; 25 points during Perfect Pairs.
